TL;DR: Practice Room does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app works entirely offline on your device. We take your privacy seriously - because we literally can't see anything you do in the app.

Overview

Practice Room ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Don't Collect

Practice Room does NOT collect any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Names or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Learning progress or preferences
  • Any form of personal data

How Practice Room Works

Practice Room operates entirely offline:

  • Local Storage Only: All app content and your preferences are stored locally on your device
  • No Network Requests: The app does not make any network requests for tracking or analytics
  • No Account System: There are no user accounts, logins, or profiles
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data stays on your device and is never uploaded anywhere

Third-Party Services

Practice Room uses Firebase solely for app configuration management. This service:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Is only used for basic app functionality
  • Does not track individual users

Children's Privacy

Practice Room is suitable for all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information from anyone, we also don't knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Data Security

Since Practice Room doesn't collect or transmit any personal data, there is no risk of your information being compromised through our app. All your preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms.

Changes to Your Data

You can clear all app data at any time by:

  • Deleting the app from your device
  • Using iOS Settings > General > iPhone Storage > Practice Room > Delete App

Updates to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@practiceroom.app

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, traditional data rights (access, deletion, portability) don't apply. However, you always have complete control over the app on your device and can remove it at any time.
